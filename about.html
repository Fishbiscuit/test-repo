About Us

Speak for Climate is an initiative borne out of a desire to see greater collaboration and transparency in the public consultation process within the environmental community in Singapore.

Accessible

Make Singapore-specific climate information accessible for citizens to use in furthering advocacy

Connect

Meet other citizens interested in similar aspects of the climate issue and form greater coalitions.

Inform

Get a better sense of what is happening in the environmental scene to understand gaps and potential synergies.

Collaborate

Strengthen the push for more ambitious climate policy as climate action must go beyond indviidual action to deal with the crisis effectively.
