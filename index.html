Speak for Climate

Borne out of a desire to see greater collaboration and transparency in the public consultation process within the environmental community in Singapore

More about us
The NCCS public consultation is a nation-wide exercise to gather feedback on Singapore climate change policy until 31st August. We hope to create a space which empowers participation by collecting individual and organisational NCCS submissions and displaying it for all to view and discuss.
